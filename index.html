FAQs

"Could we BE anymore stuck on this logic?"

Q: How many people can be on a team?
A: You can have a maximum of 6 members per team (1 Team Leader and up to 5 additional members). Just like the iconic crew at Central Perk, six is the magic number! You can also compete with a smaller team if you prefer.

Q: What if I don't have a full team yet?
A: Don't worry, you won't be stuck playing "Bamboozled" alone! You can register your team now and use the Edit feature to add more friends later.

Q: Are cross-campus or cross-year teams allowed?
A: Absolutely! Whether you are a 1st-year from IEM Saltlake or a 4th-year from IEM Newtown, you can team up. Mixing years and departments makes for the best projects.

Q: Do I need to be a coding expert to participate?
A: Not at all! Pivot Point is a "build to learn" environment. Whether you are an AI/ML wizard or just starting in frontend, everyone is welcome!

Q: Can we write code before the hackathon starts?
A: All code must be written during the official 8-hour event. You can brainstorm and create UI wireframes beforehand, but the actual coding begins when the clock starts.

Q: What should I bring to the event?
A: Bring your laptop, charger, student ID, and any specific hardware you need, and plenty of enthusiasm. You may bring your own snacks and drinks!
